<p><strong>The Automotive Polyurethane Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-component Adhesive</li><li>Two-component Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive polyurethane adhesive market includes two primary types: one-component and two-component adhesives. One-component adhesives are pre-mixed formulations that cure through moisture absorption, offering ease of use and faster application. In contrast, two-component adhesives consist of separate resin and hardener components, requiring mixing before application, leading to enhanced strength and durability. Both types are critical in automotive manufacturing and repair, providing strong bonding solutions for various materials, including metals, plastics, and composites, ensuring structural integrity and reliability in vehicles.</p></p>
