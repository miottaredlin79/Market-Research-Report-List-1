<p><p>The artificial intelligence chip market primarily consists of four types: CPUs, GPUs, ASICs, and FPGAs. CPUs (Central Processing Units) handle general tasks and can run AI algorithms but are less efficient for intensive computations. GPUs (Graphics Processing Units) excel in parallel processing, making them ideal for training complex AI models. ASICs (Application-Specific Integrated Circuits) are custom-designed for specific AI tasks, offering high efficiency. FPGAs (Field-Programmable Gate Arrays) are versatile and can be reconfigured for different applications, balancing performance and flexibility.</p></p>
