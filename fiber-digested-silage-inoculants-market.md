<p><ul><li>Homofermentative</li><li>Heterofermentative</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber-digested silage inoculants market is categorized into two main types: homofermentative and heterofermentative inoculants. Homofermentative inoculants primarily convert sugars into lactic acid, enhancing fermentation efficiency and preserving nutrients. They are favored for their ability to quickly acidify silage, promoting stability. In contrast, heterofermentative inoculants produce multiple fermentation products, including acetic acid, which can help reduceSpoilage and enhance aerobic stability. Each type plays a critical role in optimizing silage quality and improving livestock feed efficiency.</p></p>
