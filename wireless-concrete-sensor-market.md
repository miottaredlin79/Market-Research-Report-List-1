<p><p>The Wireless Concrete Sensor market is categorized into two main types: Bluetooth Transmission and Network Transmission. Bluetooth Transmission sensors utilize short-range wireless technology for direct data transfer to nearby devices, making them suitable for small-scale projects or localized monitoring. In contrast, Network Transmission sensors connect to broader networks, allowing remote data access and real-time monitoring over larger distances. This makes them ideal for extensive construction sites where comprehensive oversight and connectivity are essential for effective project management.</p></p>
