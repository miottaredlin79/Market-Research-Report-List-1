<p><strong>The Cotton Harvester Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stripper Type</li><li>Spindle Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cotton harvester market is primarily divided into two types: stripper and spindle. Stripper-type harvesters efficiently pull the entire cotton plant from the field, making them suitable for large-scale operations due to their high productivity. In contrast, spindle-type harvesters utilize rotating spindles to pick cotton bolls directly from the plants, focusing on high-quality harvesting with minimal crop damage. Both types cater to different farming needs and environments, influencing their market demand and adoption in the agricultural industry.</p></p>
