<p><p>The hydraulic cylinder market consists of various types designed for specific applications. Tie rod cylinders feature external rods connecting the end caps, offering ease of repair and maintenance. Welded cylinders are robust, with the body and end caps welded together, enhancing strength and reducing leakage. Telescopic cylinders consist of multiple nested sections, allowing for compact design and extended reach. Mill type cylinders, used in heavy-duty applications, have a larger bore and robust construction to withstand high pressures and harsh environments.</p></p>
