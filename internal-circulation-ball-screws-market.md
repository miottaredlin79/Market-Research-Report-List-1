<p><p>The internal circulation ball screws market comprises two main types: single nut and double nut configurations. Double nut ball screws, on the other hand, utilize two nuts, enhancing load capacity and stiffness, making them suitable for high-precision applications. Both types cater to varied industrial needs, ensuring smooth motion and efficient power transmission in automation and machinery.</p></p>
