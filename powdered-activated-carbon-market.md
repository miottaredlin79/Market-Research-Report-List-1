<p><p>The powdered activated carbon market can be categorized into two primary methods: physical and chemical. The physical method involves the activation of carbon through high-temperature processes, enhancing surface area and porosity, making it effective for adsorbing pollutants. In contrast, the chemical method utilizes chemical agents to treat carbon, creating specific surface properties that improve adsorption efficiency for targeted contaminants. Both methods serve various industries, including water treatment, air purification, and pharmaceuticals, addressing diverse environmental and health-related challenges.</p></p>
