<p><ul><li>In-line</li><li>End-of-line</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spelled arrestors are protective devices used to manage voltage spikes in electrical systems. The in-line market involves incorporating arrestors directly into the circuit, providing continuous protection as part of the system infrastructure. This type is typically used in applications requiring constant monitoring and immediate response to voltage fluctuations. In contrast, the end-of-line market uses arrestors at the terminal points of electrical systems, safeguarding sensitive equipment from external disturbances. This configuration is ideal for systems where additional protection is needed at endpoints.</p></p>
