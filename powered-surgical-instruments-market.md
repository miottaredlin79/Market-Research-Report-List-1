<p><p>The powered surgical instruments market is categorized into three main types: electric-powered, battery-powered, and pneumatic-powered instruments. Electric-powered instruments rely on continuous electric supply, offering consistent power for precise operations. Battery-powered instruments provide portability and convenience, making them ideal for procedures where mobility is essential. Pneumatic-powered instruments utilize compressed air for operation, offering high power-to-weight ratios, which is beneficial in various surgical settings. Each type serves distinct surgical needs, enhancing efficiency and precision in medical procedures.</p></p>
