<p><p>The gel electrophoresis apparatus market comprises various types, including agarose gel, pulse field gel, and temperature gradient gel. Agarose gel is primarily used for separating DNA and RNA fragments due to its porous structure. Pulse field gel electrophoresis allows for the separation of larger DNA molecules by applying an alternating electric field. Temperature gradient gel electrophoresis facilitates the separation of nucleic acids based on their thermal stability, enhancing resolution for specific applications. These types cater to diverse research and clinical needs in molecular biology.</p></p>
