<p><strong>The Automatic Electric Hand Dryer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Jet Air Type</li><li>Warm Air Dryer (WAD) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic electric hand dryer market comprises two primary types: Jet Air Type and Warm Air Dryer (WAD) Type. Jet Air dryers utilize high-speed air streams to quickly evaporate moisture from hands, promoting faster drying while often being energy-efficient. In contrast, Warm Air Dryers offer a gentler, slightly warmer airflow for drying hands, typically taking a bit longer but providing a more comfortable experience. Both types serve the essential purpose of enhancing hygiene and convenience in public and commercial restrooms.</p></p>
