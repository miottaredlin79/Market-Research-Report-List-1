<p><p>The gastrointestinal (GI) stents market encompasses various types designed to alleviate obstructions and improve patient quality of life. Biliary stents facilitate bile flow in cases of strictures. Esophageal stents prevent or treat dysphagia by maintaining esophageal patency. Duodenal stents address blockages in the upper intestine, aiding digestion. Pancreatic stents assist in managing pancreatic duct obstructions, while colonic stents relieve bowel obstructions, enhancing colonic function. Each stent type is tailored to specific clinical needs within the GI tract.</p></p>
