<p><p>Head motion capture systems are essential for tracking and analyzing head movements in various applications, including animation and virtual reality. The magnetic motion capture system uses magnetic fields to track movements, providing high precision but can be affected by metal interference. Mechanical motion capture relies on articulated hardware to capture movements, offering robustness but limiting flexibility. Optical motion capture employs cameras to track reflective markers, providing high accuracy and freedom of movement but requiring a clear line of sight and controlled environments.</p></p>
