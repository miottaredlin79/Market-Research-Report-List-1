<p><ul><li>MR-Guided</li><li>Ultrasound-Guided</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) devices are classified mainly into MR-guided and ultrasound-guided types. MR-guided HIFU uses magnetic resonance imaging to visualize and target tissues precisely, allowing for real-time monitoring of treatment effects and ensuring minimal damage to surrounding areas. In contrast, ultrasound-guided HIFU leverages ultrasound imaging for guidance, providing quick access and lower costs. Both types are employed in treating various conditions, including tumors, by delivering focused ultrasound waves to heat and destroy targeted tissue non-invasively.</p></p>
