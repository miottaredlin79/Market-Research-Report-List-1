<p><p>The suture needle market encompasses various types tailored for specific surgical applications. Round bodied needles are designed for soft tissues, minimizing trauma. Reverse cutting needles excel in tough tissues, while conventional cutting needles are effective for skin and fibrous structures. Each type serves unique surgical needs.</p></p>
