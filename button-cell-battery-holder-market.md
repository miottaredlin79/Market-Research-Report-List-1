<p><strong>The Button Cell Battery Holder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Mount</li><li>Through Hole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The button cell battery holder market can be categorized into surface mount and through hole types. Surface mount holders are designed to be soldered directly onto the surface of a PCB, facilitating compact designs and efficient space utilization. They are ideal for modern electronic devices where size is crucial. In contrast, through hole holders involve mounting components through drilled holes in the PCB, providing robust connections and durability, making them suitable for larger applications where reliability is paramount.</p></p>
