<p><ul><li>Manual Trigger</li><li>Thermal Trigger</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The kitchen fire suppression systems market is primarily categorized into manual trigger and thermal trigger systems. Manual trigger systems require human intervention to activate, offering flexibility and control, ideal for environments where immediate response is necessary. In contrast, thermal trigger systems use heat-sensitive mechanisms to automatically discharge extinguishing agents when temperatures reach a predetermined threshold. Both systems play crucial roles in enhancing kitchen safety, catering to different operational needs and risk levels in commercial and residential settings.</p></p>
