<p><p>Small Modular Reactors (SMRs) are compact nuclear reactors designed for flexibility, safety, and enhanced efficiency. They are categorized into two main market types: thermal-neutron reactors, which utilize slow neutrons for fission and are ideal for generating electricity and heat with a lower operational temperature, and fast-neutron reactors, which use fast neutrons to sustain the fission process, allowing for the recycling of nuclear fuel. Both types contribute to the growing demand for reliable, low-carbon energy solutions.</p></p>
