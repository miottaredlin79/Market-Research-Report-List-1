<p><strong>The Portable Gas Chromatography Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas-solid chromatography (GSC)</li><li>Gas-liquid chromatography (GLC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable gas chromatography market includes two main types: gas-solid chromatography (GSC) and gas-liquid chromatography (GLC). GSC utilizes a solid stationary phase to separate gases based on adsorption, making it ideal for applications like environmental monitoring and industrial process analysis. In contrast, GLC employs a liquid stationary phase to separate gaseous compounds based on their volatility, commonly used in chemical analysis and petrochemical applications. Both types offer portability for on-site analysis, enhancing data collection and decision-making.</p></p>
