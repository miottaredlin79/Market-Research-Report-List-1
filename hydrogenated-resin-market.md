<p><strong>The Hydrogenated Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C5</li><li>C9</li><li>DCPD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydrogenated resin market is categorized into three primary types based on their chemical structure and properties: C5, C9, and DCPD resins. C5 hydrogenated resins are derived from cyclopentadiene and are known for their light color and good adhesion properties, making them suitable for adhesives and coatings. C9 resins, derived from aromatic compounds, offer enhanced thermal stability and performance in high-temperature applications. DCPD resins, sourced from dicyclopentadiene, provide unique properties ideal for specialized applications in paints and automotive coatings.</p></p>
