<p><p>Online ordering systems can be categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based systems are hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access the software via the internet, offering scalability, automatic updates, and reduced hardware costs. On-premises systems, on the other hand, are installed locally on the user’s hardware, providing greater control and customization but requiring more substantial upfront investments and ongoing maintenance. Each type caters to different business needs and operational preferences.</p></p>
