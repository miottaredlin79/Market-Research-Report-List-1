<p><strong>The RO Water Dispenser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>POU RO Water Dispenser</li><li>POE RO Water Dispenser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RO water dispenser market is categorized into two main types: Point of Use (POU) and Point of Entry (POE) systems. POU RO water dispensers are installed at specific water access points, providing filtered water on demand for consumption and cooking. In contrast, POE RO water dispensers are connected to the main water supply, treating all incoming water before it reaches faucets and appliances. Both types serve to enhance water quality, yet cater to different needs and installation preferences.</p></p>
