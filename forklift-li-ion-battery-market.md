<p><ul><li>Lithium Ion Manganese Oxide Battery</li><li>Lithium Iron Phosphate Battery</li><li>LiNiMnCo (NMC) Battery</li><li>Lithium–titanate Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The forklift Li-Ion battery market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Lithium Ion Manganese Oxide (LiMn2O4)**: Offers high thermal stability and safety, ideal for moderate energy applications. </p><p>2. **Lithium Iron Phosphate (LiFePO4)**: Known for long life and excellent thermal stability, making it suitable for heavy-duty use. </p><p>3. **LiNiMnCo (NMC)**: Combines nickel, manganese, and cobalt for high energy density and efficiency, popular in demanding applications. </p><p>4. **Lithium–Titanate (LTO)**: Provides fast charging and extended lifespan, though with lower energy density, ideal for specialized applications.</p></p>
