<p><strong>The Linen Dryer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fluidized Bed Dryers</li><li>Conduction Dryers</li><li>Convection Dryers</li><li>Rotary Dryers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The linen dryer market comprises various types of dryers designed for efficient drying processes. Fluidized bed dryers utilize a fluidized air stream to enhance heat transfer, ensuring uniform drying. Conduction dryers rely on direct contact with heated surfaces for moisture removal. Convection dryers circulate hot air around the linen for even drying, while rotary dryers use a rotating drum to facilitate heat exchange and moisture evaporation. Each type offers distinct advantages in terms of efficiency, drying speed, and product integrity.</p></p>
