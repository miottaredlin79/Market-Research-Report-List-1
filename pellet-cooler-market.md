<p><strong>The Pellet Cooler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal Type</li><li>Vertical Type</li><li>Counterflow Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pellet cooler market comprises three main types: horizontal, vertical, and counterflow coolers. Horizontal coolers feature a design that allows pellets to move through a chamber while cooling air flows perpendicular to their movement. Vertical coolers utilize gravity to facilitate the downward flow of pellets, enhancing cooling efficiency. Counterflow coolers operate by directing cooling air upwards against the flow of pellets, promoting optimal temperature reduction. Each type offers distinct advantages for various industrial applications, impacting efficiency and operational costs.</p></p>
