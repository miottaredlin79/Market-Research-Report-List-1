<p><strong>The Heat Sensitive Adhesive Label Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-reversible</li><li>Reversible</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heat sensitive adhesive label market comprises two primary types: non-reversible and reversible labels. Non-reversible labels change color or lose adhesive properties upon exposure to heat, making them ideal for tamper-evident applications and ensuring product security. In contrast, reversible labels maintain their adhesive qualities and can be reactivated with heat, allowing for repositionability and reuse. Each type serves distinct purposes, catering to different industries such as logistics, pharmaceuticals, and food packaging, depending on the desired functionality and end use.</p></p>
