<p><p>High-nickel batteries are categorized into three main types: NCA (Nickel-Cobalt-Aluminum), NMC (Nickel-Manganese-Cobalt), and NCMA (Nickel-Cobalt-Manganese-Aluminum). NCA batteries excel in energy density, making them ideal for electric vehicles. NMC offers a balanced performance with good thermal stability and cycle life, suitable for various applications. NCMA combines the benefits of NMC with enhanced thermal management and lower cobalt content, reducing costs and environmental impact. Together, these technologies are driving advancements in energy storage and electric mobility.</p></p>
