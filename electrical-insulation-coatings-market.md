<p><p>Electrical insulation coatings are essential for protecting electrical components from environmental and operational stresses. **Water-borne coatings** use water as a solvent, offering eco-friendly, low-VOC solutions with easy application and cleanup. **Solvent-borne coatings** rely on organic solvents, providing superior durability and resistance but with higher VOC emissions. **Powder-based coatings** are applied as dry powder and cured under heat, delivering excellent insulation, durability, and environmental benefits with minimal waste. Each type caters to specific performance, environmental, and application needs in the electrical insulation market.</p></p>
