<p><p>The carpet and rugs market features several types, each defined by its construction method. Woven carpets are crafted on looms, offering durability and intricate patterns. Tufted carpets are made by inserting fibers into a backing material, allowing for quicker production and a variety of styles. Knotted rugs, often handcrafted, are known for their detailed designs and can be quite durable. Needle-punched carpets are produced by entangling fibers through a backing, making them suitable for industrial applications due to their strength and low cost.</p></p>
