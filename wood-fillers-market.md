<p><ul><li>Solvent-Based</li><li>Water-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wood fillers market can be categorized into solvent-based and water-based products. Solvent-based wood fillers offer excellent adhesion and quick drying times, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. They are often used in outdoor projects due to their durability and resistance to moisture. In contrast, water-based wood fillers are more environmentally friendly, easy to clean, and suitable for interior applications. They have lower VOC emissions and provide a smoother finish, making them ideal for fine woodworking and furniture restoration.</p></p>
