<p><p>The flexible partial denture market can be categorized into two primary types: single dentures and multiple dentures. Single dentures are designed to replace one or a few missing teeth, providing a more affordable and less invasive solution for patients. In contrast, multiple dentures cater to patients missing several teeth across different areas of the mouth, offering enhanced stability and functionality. Both types focus on comfort and aesthetics, using flexible materials that adapt to the natural contours of the gums and teeth.</p></p>
