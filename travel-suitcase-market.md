<p><p>The travel suitcase market can be categorized into two main types: hard-sided and soft-edge travel cases. Hard-sided travel cases offer superior protection for fragile items, featuring rigid exteriors made from durable materials like polycarbonate or aluminum. These cases are often waterproof and tamper-resistant. In contrast, soft-edge travel cases utilize flexible fabrics, providing lightweight and expandability for packing convenience. They typically include external pockets for easy access and are favored for casual travel, balancing portability and storage.</p></p>
