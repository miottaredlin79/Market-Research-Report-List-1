<p><p>The HVAC pump market includes various types designed for different applications. Booster pumps increase water pressure to ensure effective circulation in heating and cooling systems. Circulating pumps maintain consistent fluid movement, enhancing system efficiency and comfort by distributing heated or cooled water. Boiler feed pumps supply water to boilers, ensuring optimal operation and preventing dry running. Each type plays a crucial role in maintaining HVAC system performance, energy efficiency, and overall comfort in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
