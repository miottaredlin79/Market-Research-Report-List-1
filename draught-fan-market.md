<p><p>The draught fan market can be categorized into low-pressure and high-pressure fans, each serving distinct industrial needs. Low-pressure fans are designed for applications requiring large volumes of air at lower resistance, typically used for ventilation and cooling purposes. In contrast, high-pressure fans deliver air at significantly higher velocities and are suited for processes that demand greater airflow resistance, such as combustion and pneumatic conveying. Both types play critical roles in enhancing efficiency across various sectors, including manufacturing and energy.</p></p>
