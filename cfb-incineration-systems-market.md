<p><p>CFB (Circulating Fluidized Bed) incineration systems are categorized based on operational pressure and thermal efficiency. Supercritical systems operate above critical pressure, enhancing combustion efficiency and reducing emissions. Subcritical systems function below critical pressure, offering lower efficiency but cost benefits. Ultra high-pressure systems achieve very high pressures for optimized performance, while high-pressure systems balance efficiency and operational costs. Other types include variations tailored for specific waste streams or operational requirements, each designed to maximize energy recovery and minimize environmental impact.</p></p>
