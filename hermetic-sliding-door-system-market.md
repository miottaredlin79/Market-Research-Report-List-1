<p><p>The Hermetic Sliding Door System market is categorized into two primary types: Single Leaf and Double Leaf systems. Single Leaf systems consist of one door panel that slides to open or close, ideal for smaller spaces requiring efficient access and airtight closure. In contrast, Double Leaf systems feature two door panels that slide apart, providing a wider entrance suitable for high-traffic areas or larger openings. Both types are designed to ensure superior airtightness and enhanced energy efficiency in various applications.</p></p>
