<p><p>The Transparent TV market can be categorized into two types: Variable Transparency and Fixed Transparency. Variable Transparency TVs allow users to adjust the level of transparency, enabling them to switch between a completely opaque display and varying degrees of see-through functionality. In contrast, Fixed Transparency TVs have a set transparency level that cannot be altered, offering a consistent viewing experience where the display remains semi-transparent at all times. Both types cater to different user preferences and applications in interior design and advertising.</p></p>
