<p><strong>The Oil-flooded Compressor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary Screw Compressor</li><li>Reciprocating Compressor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil-flooded compressor market is primarily divided into two types: rotary screw compressors and reciprocating compressors. Rotary screw compressors utilize two interlocking screws to compress air, providing continuous flow and high efficiency, making them ideal for industrial applications. In contrast, reciprocating compressors use pistons driven by a crankshaft to compress air in cycles, offering high pressure but lower flow rates. Both types are essential in various industries for air supply, refrigeration, and vacuum applications, with unique benefits suited to different operational needs.</p></p>
