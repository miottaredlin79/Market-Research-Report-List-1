<p><p>Mixed flow dryers are categorized into stationary and mobile markets based on their design and application. Stationary mixed flow dryers are fixed installations, typically used in large-scale operations for consistent drying of materials, maximizing throughput and efficiency. In contrast, mobile mixed flow dryers are portable units, offering flexibility and convenience for smaller operations or for areas with space constraints.
