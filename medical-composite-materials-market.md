<p><p>The medical composite materials market encompasses various types, including fiber composites, polymer-metal composites, and polymer-ceramic composites. Fiber composites utilize reinforcing fibers within a polymer matrix, enhancing strength and flexibility for applications like prosthetics. Polymer-metal composites combine polymers with metals to achieve improved mechanical properties and corrosion resistance, making them suitable for surgical instruments. Polymer-ceramic composites integrate ceramics with polymers, offering biocompatibility and wear resistance, ideal for dental implants and orthopedic devices, thereby advancing medical technologies and patient care.</p></p>
