<p><p>The cinematographic cameras market is categorized into three main types: ENG (Electronic News Gathering) cameras, cinema cameras, and EFP (Electronic Field Production) cameras. ENG cameras are compact and designed for news coverage, offering quick setup and portability. Cinema cameras are high-end systems used for filmmaking, characterized by superior image quality and advanced features for creative visual storytelling. EFP cameras bridge the gap, providing broadcast-quality solutions for events and programs, balancing flexibility and functionality in production environments.</p></p>
