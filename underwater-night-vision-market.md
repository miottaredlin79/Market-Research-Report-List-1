<p><p>The underwater night vision market comprises three main types: image intensifier night vision, thermal image night vision, and digital night vision. Image intensifier night vision enhances available light for improved visibility in dark conditions. Thermal image night vision detects heat signatures, making it ideal for spotting warm objects in complete darkness. Digital night vision captures and processes images electronically, offering flexibility in recording and sharing visuals. Each type serves specific applications, enhancing underwater exploration, security, and research capabilities.</p></p>
