<p><p>The GNSS IC market is divided into two main types: high precision GNSS chips and standard precision GNSS chips. High precision GNSS chips are designed for applications requiring accuracy within centimeters, often utilized in autonomous vehicles, surveying, and precision agriculture. Each type caters to distinct market needs based on performance requirements and application scenarios.</p></p>
