<p><p>The Paper & Paperboard Packaging market includes various types designed for specific applications. Solid Bleached Sulfate (SBS) is a premium quality board ideal for graphics and packaging. Coated Unbleached Kraft (CUK) offers strength and durability for heavier items. Folding Box Board (FBB) is flexible and lightweight, suitable for consumer goods. White Line Chipboard (WLC) is cost-effective for secondary packaging. Glassine & Greaseproof Paper are moisture-resistant, often used in food packaging. Label Paper provides a smooth surface for printing, ideal for branding and information.</p></p>
