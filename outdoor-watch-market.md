<p><strong>The Outdoor Watch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Quartz Movement</li><li>Mechanical Movement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The outdoor watch market is typically divided into two main types based on movement: quartz and mechanical. Quartz movement watches operate using a battery and a quartz crystal, offering accuracy and low maintenance, making them ideal for outdoor activities. In contrast, mechanical movement watches rely on intricate gears and springs, prized for craftsmanship and traditional appeal. These watches require regular winding, adding a nostalgic element. Both types cater to outdoor enthusiasts, each offering unique features to enhance outdoor experiences.</p></p>
