<p><p>The buzzer driver chip market encompasses two main types: electromagnetic and piezoelectric buzzers. Electromagnetic buzzers operate using a magnetic field to create sound, known for their rich tone and ability to produce lower frequencies, making them suitable for alarms and notifications. On the other hand, piezoelectric buzzers utilize piezoelectric materials to generate sound through mechanical vibrations, offering compact sizes and efficient energy use, ideal for applications like mobile devices and consumer electronics. Both technologies cater to diverse audio signaling needs.</p></p>
