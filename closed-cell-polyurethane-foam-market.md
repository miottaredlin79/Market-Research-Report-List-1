<p><p>The closed cell polyurethane foam market is primarily divided into two types: flexible foam and rigid foam. Flexible foam is characterized by its softness and elasticity, making it ideal for cushioning applications, such as in furniture and bedding. Rigid foam, on the other hand, is denser and offers excellent thermal insulation, commonly used in construction for insulation panels and refrigeration. Both types leverage the unique closed cell structure, providing enhanced durability, moisture resistance, and sound absorption.</p></p>
