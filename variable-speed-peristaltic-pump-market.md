<p><p>The variable speed peristaltic pump market includes two main types: peristaltic tube pumps and peristaltic hose pumps. Peristaltic tube pumps utilize flexible tubing to move fluids, allowing for precise flow rates and sterile applications, making them ideal for medical and laboratory uses. In contrast, peristaltic hose pumps feature a rugged design with reinforced hoses, suitable for handling abrasive and viscous materials in industrial settings. Both types offer versatility and efficiency, catering to diverse industries like food processing, pharmaceuticals, and wastewater management.</p></p>
